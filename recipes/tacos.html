Tacos

Delicious mexican street style tacos with green sauce and cilantro served on a styrafoam plate with a lemon,radish, and a red salsa cup

Taco recipe overview

The meat is marinated in a mexican beer along with lemon and other spices and cilantro of course this is done in a plastic bag, buy salsa from local mexican grocery store if not try to use picante brand salsa instead XD. Cook meat , cook tortillas in lard and then plate the tortillas, add meat on top, and finally add the salsa and cilantro.

taco ingredients

  • flank steak
  • modelo beer
  • spices and herbs
  • lard
  • tortillas
  • salsa

taco cooking steps

  1. marinate steak in beer and herb/spice mix
  2. cook meat on grill make sure its hot
  3. heat tortillas in lard until desired crispiness is achieved
  4. put tortillas on plate add steak on top
  5. add salsa and cilantro on top of the meat and tortillas
  6. tacos achieved
