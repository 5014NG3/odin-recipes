Chicken nugget recipe

The meat is ground, then forced under high pressure through screens that reduce the meat, bones, and other parts into a paste. This paste is mixed with chicken skin and additives, including starch and sodium phosphate, and then shaped into nuggets. Mix buttermilk, tomato bouillon, sugar, garlic powder and paprika in a large container with lid. Cut up chicken into 1â€³ cubes and place in buttermilk mixture. Allow chicken pieces to marinate in fridge overnight or for at least 4 hours

Ingredients for recipe

  • chicken breast
  • flour
  • spices
  • peanut oil
  • buttermilk
  • eggs

cooking steps

  1. cut chicken breast
  2. marinate in buttermilk, spices, and egg mix
  3. coat marinated chicken pieces in flour seasoned with spices
  4. fry in peanut oil at 350
  5. set on metal cooling rack
